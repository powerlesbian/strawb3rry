Common questions

I forgot my vault passphrase. Unfortunately the passphrase cannot be recovered — it never leaves your device. You will need to delete and recreate your vault from Settings.

My data isn't syncing. Check your internet connection and try signing out and back in from Settings.

How do I delete my account? Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.
