Strawb3rry Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Strawb3rry collects your email address solely to create and authenticate your account. We do not collect your name, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

Your content

Projects, ideas, and notes are stored in our database associated with your account. Secrets (API keys, passwords, env vars) are encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before being stored. Your master passphrase never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We cannot read your secrets.

Third parties

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. We do not share your data with any other third parties. We do not serve ads. We do not use tracking or analytics SDKs.

Data deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings tab in the app. This permanently deletes all your data from our servers.
